Privacy, Terms, and Data Notes

Market Sweep is a lightweight market-news and stock-research tool. It is provided for informational and educational use only.

Not Investment Advice

Nothing on this site is financial, investment, tax, or legal advice. Market data, headlines, rankings, scenario math, and chart notes can be delayed, incomplete, or wrong. Always verify information before making financial decisions.

Data Sources and Copyright

News links point to third-party publishers. Headlines, publisher names, and links remain owned by their respective publishers. Market and company data may be derived from public SEC filings and market-data endpoints such as Yahoo Finance. Market Sweep is not affiliated with or endorsed by those data providers or publishers.

Use the original publisher links for full articles and licensing terms. Paywalled sources are labeled when detected, but access rights are controlled by the publisher.

Privacy

  • No account signup is offered.
  • No personal information is intentionally collected through the site UI.
  • No advertising trackers or analytics scripts are intentionally embedded.
  • The stock page may store a chart-display preference in your browser localStorage.
  • Hosting and network providers may process ordinary server logs, such as IP address, browser metadata, requested URL, and timestamp, for security and operations.

Acceptable Use

Do not scrape, overload, reverse engineer, or use the site to bypass third-party access controls. The site may be changed, rate-limited, or removed at any time.

Corrections

If something looks wrong, verify against the original source first. Market Sweep favors source links and visible data notes so errors can be traced quickly.
